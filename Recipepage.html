Magic Peanut Butter Cookies

Try this easy to make cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup Granulated sugar
  • 1 whole Egg
  • Lots of love

Instructions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350degrees
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl
  3. Use a cookie scoop and drop dough on a baking sheet
  4. Bake for 10mins
